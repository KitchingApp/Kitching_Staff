#### 🔪프랩

- 날짜에 따라 해야할 프랩을 관리하는 화면

#### 🍳레시피
<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/47f02549-6454-4bae-b9bc-1888ae73f2f1" width="200px" /> <img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6b31dee1-b59d-4c05-88d3-a72924fda831" width="200px" />

#### 🗓️스케줄
<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bc59f216-c4ee-4fc4-bb7d-d38d9b918d7c" width="200px" /> <img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/74cc73b3-1553-4ed0-be26-c9db27bd65e3" width="200px" />

#### 📑발주목록

- 발주해야할 품목을 한눈에 확인 후 발주할 수 있는 화면

#### 📌그 외

<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f1f223ee-a044-4578-80cd-d2e5b6ad22e2" width="200px" /> <img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5a9ecd0f-1116-443d-b7f5-a2fde3809e67" width="200px" /> <img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b7b046b0-3c20-4a80-9f69-5e5f2ff0f5c2" width="200px" />
